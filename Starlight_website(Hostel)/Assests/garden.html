Our hostel's lush green garden offers a tranquil escape from the busy city and college life. With carefully curated plants, vibrant flowers, and serene seating areas, it's the perfect spot for relaxation, study or social gatherings. The garden is accessible to all hostel residents and is a cherished part of our community.
