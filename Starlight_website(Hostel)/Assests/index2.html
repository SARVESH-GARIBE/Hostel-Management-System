ABOUT US

Welcome there ! Starlight hostel, located in the heart of city Amravati,is a vibrant and social hostel that offers a unique and unforgettable experience for students.


Our hostel is designed to provide a comfortable,clean,safe environment to students with a range of amenities and services to make their stay truly enjoyable.


We offer a variety of room types to suit all the budgets and preferences including dormitory rooms with 3-4 beds and rooms with 2 beds facility equipped with lockers , free wifi access , comfortable beds and fresh linens with study tables at each bed.


We offers a range of amenities to make student stay with us comfortable and convenient including with proper and hygienic food , freshly prepared in our kitchens with weekly fixed menu and special dishes on various days , 24 hour reception and security, outdoor patio area for relaxation and socializing, Laundry facilities, Free city maps and many more services .


Our hostel is social and vibrant space , perfect for meeting new people and making friends..We organise various events and activities including movie nights , game nights. , cultural events and workshop with promotions to outdoor games and excursion...
